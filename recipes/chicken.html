Chicken

This is the only crispy fried chicken recipe you'll ever need. Why? It combines all the tried-and-true tricks to ensure the juiciest, most flavorful, crispiest chicken every time.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
    • four-pound whole chicken into pieces or purchase four pounds of breasts, drumsticks, wings, legs, and/or thighs at the grocery store.
  • Buttermilk
    • Acidic buttermilk tenderizes the chicken without making it too tough. Alio, it helps the flour mixture stick to the chicken.
  • Flour
    • All-purpose flour gives the buttermilk and seasonings something to stick to, while ensuring a wonderfully crispy crust.
  • Seasoning
    • This crispy fried chicken recipe calli for paprika (which helps with browning), salt, and pepper. You can add more spices and seasonings to taste.
  • Oil
    • Vegetable oil is perfect for frying chicken because it has a high smoke point.

Steps:

  1. Step 1.
    2. Take your cut up chicken pieces and skin them if you prefer.
  2. Step 2.
    3. Put the flour in a large plastic bag (let the amount of chicken you are cooking dictate the amount of flour you use). Season the flour with paprika, salt and pepper to taste (paprika helps to brown the chicken).
  3. Step 3.
    4. Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk then, a few at a time, put them in the bag with the flour, seal the bag and shake to coat well.
  4. Step 4.
    5. Place the coated chicken on a cookie sheet or tray, and cover with a clean dish towel or waxed paper. LET SIT UNTIL THE FLOUR IS OF A PASTE-LIKE CONSISTENCY. THIS IS CRUCIAL!
